Eggs

Description

Eggs are perfect breakfast nutrition. They are a good source of proteins and vitamins. That's why the old saying is: "Every morning one egg gives the body strength." Eggs are the best cheap nutrition you could get!

Eggs can be made various ways. You can make egg: omelets, quiche, boiled eggs, scrambled eggs, frittata, eggs on the eye, and many more ways. This page will show you how to make the best scrambled eggs you ever tried.

Ingridients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tea spoon of Vegeta
  • dried sousages or bacon

Steps

  1. break eggs and add Vegeta to them
  2. scramble eggs with Vegeta
  3. put the bacon on hot pan
  4. after bacon relised the juices put the onion and pepper on pan
  5. at least add scrambled eggs to the pan
