Beef Neck

Description

Beef neck is one of my favorite meat meals. It is full of proteins and healthy fat which gives you energy. Althought it is super foodstuff you should not eat it too much. It could make your cholesterol go high. Any way beef neck is one of my favorite foods, and it is okay to eat it twice a week.

Beef neck can be made various ways, imagination is the only limit. I like it simple, so mostly I grill the neck with as little spices as possible. It can be made grilled rare, medium rare, medium, medium well or well. Personally I like it most when it's medium well or well.

Ingridients

  • beef neck
  • salt (or Vegeta)
  • black papper

Steps

  1. marinade the beef neck with salt and black pepper
  2. leave it in marinade for at least 3 hours
  3. after the neck absorbed marinade, put it on hot pan
  4. depending on how you like it the most grill the neck for few minutes
  5. well grilled beef neck usually takes up to 10 minutes
